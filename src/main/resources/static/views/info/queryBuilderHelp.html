This component facilitates the exploration and discovery of semantic metadata. It incorporates a multitude of features on top of an intuitive and user friendly environment, in order for both novice and expert users to execute complex queries.

In particular, it allows users to construct both simple and advanced (SPARQL) queries, through an easy and friendly environment, aiming at supporting them during this knowledge discovery process. The main concept of query construction follows a general pattern, according to which end-users who wish to search for a particular entity can start with partial information regarding this entity, but can narrow down their search space through appropriate refinements on other related entities, for which information is known.
